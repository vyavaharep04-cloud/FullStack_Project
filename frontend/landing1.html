About FinanTra

FinanTra is a personal finance management web application designed to help users track their income, monitor expenses, and understand their financial habits. The platform provides simple tools like transaction tracking, analytics dashboards, and budgeting features to help individuals make smarter financial decisions.

Problem

Many people struggle to manage their finances effectively because they lack a clear view of their income, spending patterns, and savings. Without proper tracking tools, financial planning becomes difficult.

Our Solution

FinanTra provides a simple and intuitive platform where users can record transactions, analyze spending patterns through visual dashboards, and plan their budgets effectively.

Technology

Frontend built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for an interactive UI. Backend integration with Node.js and MongoDB is planned for full-stack functionality.
