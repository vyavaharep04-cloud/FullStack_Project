General

Features

FinanTra provides a range of tools including:

  • 💳 Expense and income tracking with categories
  • 📊 Analytics dashboard with charts
  • 📅 Budget Planner to set monthly limits
  • 📈 Financial Health score
  • 📄 SOA Statement download
  • 💱 Currency Converter
  • 📆 Monthly Comparison tool

The analytics dashboard shows your financial data through visual charts. You can switch between Weekly, Monthly, and Yearly views. The pie chart shows expense breakdown by category while the bar chart compares income vs expenses over time.

SOA stands for Statement of Account. This feature allows you to generate and download a detailed summary of all your transactions for a selected time period, similar to a bank statement. It helps you keep records of your financial activity.

Security

FinanTra is designed to keep your financial data private and secure. User data is stored only for the purpose of providing the service and is not shared with third parties.

Currently, FinanTra stores transaction data locally in your browser using localStorage. This means your data stays on your device. When the backend is fully integrated, data will be stored securely on a server with MongoDB.

No. Each user's financial data is private and accessible only to them. Other users cannot view your transactions, categories, or account information. Your data belongs only to you.
